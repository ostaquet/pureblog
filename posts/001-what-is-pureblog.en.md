---
title: What is Pureblog?
date: 2026-05-06
excerpt: Pureblog is a static and minimalist blog engine with excellent support for multilingual content.
---

Pureblog is a static and minimalist blog engine with enhanced support for content written in multiple languages. Pureblog converts content written in Markdown to a static website.

It has no trackers and no lines of Javascript. The design is deliberately minimalist. By design, Pureblog is extremely fast and requires very few system resources to host your blog even if you have significant traffic.

**The only thing that truly matters with your content.**
